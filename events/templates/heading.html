What's happening in Nevada County?

Welcome to The List NC! Feeling inspired by Graham Spencer's The List, we decided to build a public event database for Nevada County, CA. From festivals and farmer's markets to trivia nights and live performances, there's something for everyone.

The database here is maintained by a team of local volunteers and we do our best to make sure the information provided is accurate and up-to-date. If you're interested in contributing or see any inaccurate information, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at hello@thelistnc.org. We're friendly, I promise. Your support is greatly appreciated and we hope you enjoy your visit.
