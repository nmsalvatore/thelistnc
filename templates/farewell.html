Dear friends,

After a lot of consideration, I've decided to shut this project down.

I've long wanted something like The List NC to exist in Nevada County, something similar to The List for the bay area, but with a wider scope than just music. A single, free resource where people can go and see what's happening in the community.

While I still believe in this vision, I find myself unable to give it the attention it deserves and feel the need to step away. Over the last 8 months or so, a number of people have reached out to me expressing interest in doing something similar. I encourage you to do so. The community has expressed a real need for it.

The contact email hello@thelistnc.org will remain active until the end of February. If you have any questions for me, feel free to get in touch.

Thank you so much for your support and I wish you all the best in 2025.

Nick, The List NC
